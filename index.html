Engagement Tiers

The Brief

Starting at $15,000

One bounded question, decision-ready memo with evidence-weighted confidence scoring and clear recommendation.

Best for: Go/no-go decisions, competitive exposure assessments, market entry gut-checks

~2 week timeline

  • "What's our competitive exposure if Amazon Pharmacy expands into specialty?"
  • "Is there a viable acquisition target in [adjacent market] under $50M?"
  • "What's the real margin risk if [payer] changes reimbursement terms?"

The Analysis

Starting at $25,000

Complex question, full landscape analysis, strategy + implementation roadmap. Everything in The Brief plus: competitive landscape mapping, scenario modeling, implementation sequencing, and strategic debrief.

Best for: Decisions that change how you operate — market entry, business model shifts, competitive repositioning

  • "Should we re-enter DME — and if so, through what model and in what sequence?"
  • "How should we position against vertical integration from [competitor] over the next 18 months?"
  • "What's the build-vs-buy-vs-partner calculus for launching [new service line]?"

Best for: Teams facing recurring strategic questions who want each answer to build on the last

Full sprint engagements scoped separately. Advisory clients get priority scheduling and compounding intelligence.
